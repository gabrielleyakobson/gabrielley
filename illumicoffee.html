illumicoffee

The traditional equipment used to brew pour-over coffee is bulky and expensive. We designed a lightweight, collapsible dripper made entirely out of paperboard to provide a convenient alternative within the expanding market. The product needed strong branding to match its compelling, pyramid-shaped exterior, as well as an infographic to explain how its used.

prototype made in collaboration with chris tom and ryan amesbury.
